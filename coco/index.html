  • Converts .cas (Cassette) data files to .wav to play into a real Color Computer Works well depnding on sound hardware.. Seems to work well on most PCs audio out with sound on very high without distortion.. If it don't work on one, try anouther. Does not seem to work on my Samsung Phones/Tablets.. Maybe sound is not loud enough? Currently no options or validation to keep it as simple as posible.. only supports 9600 bit rate. Not sure other rates would help for other audio hardware?
